Call of Ranch is a merge tower defence game. The farm is under attack from Nature's Seasons Bosses! Defend the farm from the incoming hordes of enemies. Each enemy requiers a different approach since each have unique stats. To match monsters you have a squad of characters that function as tower,  also each with own specialty! 
Basic, Icy, Poison, Agile, Accurate and more types of towers for you summon, merge and level up in order to defeat the enemies.

To restore peace of the farm you need to defeat all four seasonal bosses, to take them down they need to be defeated three times, so in total there are twelve levels with twenty waves each!

The levels are split into two phases: rest and combat. While you're getting ready, use your summons, combine towers and move them around. During the combat phase, gather insight on enemy's weaknesses and use bonus items to complete the level. A heart to restore farms toughness, and a bomb to damage enemies. .

Also upgrade towers outside of the levels to make them even stronger, pick whichever you like to fit your playstyle!
